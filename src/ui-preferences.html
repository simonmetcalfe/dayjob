When removing a track with CTRL + ALT + -, Spotify will skip to the next track without holding the SHIFT key. Holding SHIFT then has the reverse effect and prevents skipping.
When adding tracks to playlists with CTRL + ALT + ##, they will be removed from the source playlist without holding the SHIFT key. Holding SHIFT then has the reverse effect and copys the track.
